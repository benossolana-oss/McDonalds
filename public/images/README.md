# Custom Images Folder

## Background Image
Upload your custom background image here and name it `background.jpg` or `background.png`

The website will automatically use this image as the main background.

## Chip Logo
Upload your chip logo image and name it `chip-logo.jpg` or `chip-logo.png`

This will appear next to the MCDONALDS MEMECOIN title.

## Video for Burger of the Day
Upload your video file and name it `burger-video.mp4` (or .webm, .mov)

This will replace the static image in the "Burger of the Day" section.

## Floating Image Squares
Upload up to 10 images for the floating squares:
- `float1.jpg` or `float1.png`
- `float2.jpg` or `float2.png`
- `float3.jpg` or `float3.png`
- `float4.jpg` or `float4.png`
- `float5.jpg` or `float5.png`
- `float6.jpg` or `float6.png`
- `float7.jpg` or `float7.png`
- `float8.jpg` or `float8.png`
- `float9.jpg` or `float9.png`
- `float10.jpg` or `float10.png`

These will float around the page as animated squares!

## Supported formats:
- .jpg
- .jpeg  
- .png
- .gif
- .webp
- .mp4 (for videos)
- .webm (for videos)
- .mov (for videos)

## Recommended sizes:
- Background: At least 1920x1080
- Chip Logo: 200x200px square
- Video: Any size (will auto-fit)
- Floating squares: 300x300px square

## How to use:
1. Upload your images/video to this folder
2. Name them exactly as specified above
3. The website will automatically detect and use them!
